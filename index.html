ABOUT US

We are on the beautiful, tropical island of Ibeju Lekki

We opened our Resort over 2 months ago and we are proud to have worked with the local Ibeju Lekki community from then and till today. We still source most of our foodstuffs and building materials from the nearby villages

Lagos Beach Hotel is situated on the beautiful tropical island of Ibeju Lekki. Our hotel is situated on a picture perfect beach with white powdery sands, turquoise waters and shady palm trees – the perfect holiday destination!

  • We pride ourselves on running one of Zanzibar’s most relaxing hotels with a stress free environment in a stunning location
  • Our restaurant offers a variety of meals with fresh seafood and fish available daily; while still being small enough to cater for all dietary requirements
  • Our bar stocks local and imported drinks as well as making daily fresh juices and cocktails – perfect for enjoying next to the inifinity pool
  1. Through working with the villagers we have developed a variety of excursions and trips throughout the island including on our own house reef just offshore
  2. And visiting the spice plantations which made Zanzibar what it is today

Wake up looking forward to the day ahead in one of our standard, superior, deluxe rooms or suites. Our standard rooms are cozy and traditional, while the others have minimalist appeal, with natural light and elegant decor inspired by the ocean and nature. All come with free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TV, safe, hairdryer and minibar. Most also have tea and coffee-making facilities.
